## Project Overview

This is a Hindi-language long-format audiobook story titled **"Mafia King: Hidden Bloodline"** (माफिया किंग: हिडन ब्लडलाइन), written for the Pocket FM platform. The story is planned for 600-700 episodes across 6 seasons.

**Premise:** Uday Singh, a poor son-in-law living in a rich Jaipur haveli, discovers he is the son of the city's most powerful don, Vikrant Singh, who was murdered by rival Ashwin Malik. The story follows his transformation from a humiliated household member to the Mafia King.

  - `सीजन/` — Season-by-season episode outlines (Season 1: 150 eps, Seasons 2-4: 125 eps each, Seasons 5-6: 100 eps each)

## Writing Rules

- **Language:** Hindi (Devanagari script only, never Roman/transliterated Hindi)
- **Format:** Novel prose style, NOT screenplay/dialogue format. All dialogue must be woven into narration since the audiobook is narrated by a single voice.
- **Episode length:** Each episode must be 3000+ words (current chapters average ~1000-1900 words — below target)
- **Episode structure:** Start with a recap of previous events, end with a preview/hook for the next episode
- **Tone:** Modern conversational Hindi, not literary/formal language
- **Engagement:** Every episode needs a high point and a strong cliffhook. Small conflict per episode, big conflict every ~10 episodes.
- **First 3 episodes** are critical for audience retention — must have mass appeal and be highly engaging.
- **Characters:** Keep introducing new characters over time for sustained engagement. Character details are in `आउटलाइन/List of Characters.txt`.

## Key Characters

- **Uday Singh** — Protagonist, Vikrant's hidden heir
- **Kiyara Singh** — Uday's wife, his emotional strength
- **Vikrant Singh** — Uday's father, former Mafia King (deceased)
- **Jagga Tiger** — Vikrant's loyal man, Uday's mentor/trainer
- **Ashwin Malik** — Main villain, Vikrant's killer
- **Bhanumati Devi** — Uday's mother-in-law, chief antagonist at home
- **Manmohan Singh** — Uday's father-in-law, spineless figurehead
- **Vikas Singh** — Uday's brother-in-law, comic relief/troublemaker
